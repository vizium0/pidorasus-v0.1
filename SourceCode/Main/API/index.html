Aeonic Language

Only studied by the most experienced of scholars, Aeonic is the tongue of the mysterious peoples who lived ages ago on Elysium. The understanding scholars have is derived from fragmentary ruins and texts pieced together across centuries of accumulated knowledge. There appear to be similarities in the language to a variety of modern language families such as Imperial and Elder, but even so the script is hard to decipher.
